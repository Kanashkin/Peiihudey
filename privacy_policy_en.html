Privacy Policy

Eat & Lose Weight App

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Eat & Lose Weight app handles user information.

1. Data Collection

Eat & Lose Weight does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data is stored exclusively on the user's device.

2. HealthKit Data

The app requests access to Apple Health (HealthKit) to read the following information:

  • Step count
  • Active calories

This data is used solely for display purposes within the app. HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, uploaded to servers, or used for advertising.

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Profile parameters (gender, age, height, weight, activity level)
  • Daily food and activity logs
  • Day history
  • Weight history

All data is stored locally on the device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

4. AI Food Scanner (Premium)

When using the AI food scanner feature, a photo of food is sent to a secure server for AI-powered analysis. The image is used solely to determine calorie content and is not stored on the server after processing.

5. Premium Subscription

Subscriptions are processed via RevenueCat. Payment data is handled by Apple through the App Store and is not shared with the app developer.

6. Notifications

The app may send local push notifications as reminders. Notifications are generated on-device and do not require any data transmission to servers.

7. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with advertising networks and does not share data with third parties, except as described in the AI Scanner section.

8. Age Requirements

The app is intended for users 18 years of age and older and does not collect data from children.

9. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be communicated through an app update.
